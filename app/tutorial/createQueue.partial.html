The work queue serves as a list of tasks for the workers to pull and process.

Each worker will pull one task, process and complete it, and upload the results before pulling the next. Once the queue is empty, the workers will terminate themselves at which point the job will be complete.

  1. To create a new queue, click the icon
  2. You'll be presented with a dialog prompting for a name for your new queue. Enter a name of your choosing and click OK
It may take up to 60 seconds for the queue to appear in the drop down list, once it does, select it
