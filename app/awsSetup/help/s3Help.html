S3 Locations

Configuring S3 locations requires a total of 2 buckets, one serves as the source and one as the destination. The source is where the blender project will be placed for the workers to pull. The destination is where the frames will be placed once the workers have processed each task in the queue.

Source Bucket

Destination Bucket

The destination bucket is where the workers will place the completed frames. The bucket can be created by simply logging into AWS, clicking S3 and clicking the "Create Bucket" button and following the prompts.

Once the bucket is created, populate the "Frame Destination Bucket" field with the bucket in the format:
s3://BUCKET_NAME
Where BUCKET_NAME is the name of the destination bucket.
